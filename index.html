How the Signals Work

Action is the current operation recommendation. Action Score measures how strongly the model supports executing that action, after price position, ranges, trend, volume, fundamentals, valuation, options, and market context are combined.

Example: Hold / Watch can have a high Action Score when the stock is between buy and sell ranges and neither side has enough edge.
